The printer has 3 cartesian axis.

The Hotbed on the Y-Axis is connected with a toothed belt to a stepper at the rear end.
With a 3D-printed part on the front

The Hotend on the x-Axis is also connected with a toothed belt to a stepper on the left side.
here is a belt-tensioner on the right side

The Z-Axis is driven by a trapezoidal thread.
